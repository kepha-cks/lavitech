FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build a school management system?

Depending on the scope, a standard school management system takes 8–16 weeks from discovery to launch. Complex multi-campus deployments may take longer. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Absolutely. Every project comes with 12 months of free support and maintenance. After that, we offer flexible support packages tailored to your needs — from basic bug fixes to full managed services.

Can your software integrate with existing systems?

Yes. We build our systems with APIs that can integrate with accounting software, payment gateways (MTN Mobile Money, Visa, etc.), biometric devices, government reporting systems, and more.

What is the pricing model?

We offer both one-time licensing and subscription-based models depending on the solution. We provide transparent quotes after the discovery phase — no hidden fees. Contact us for a custom proposal.
